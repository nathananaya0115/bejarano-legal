SMS Privacy Policy

Bejarano Insurance Agency LLC · Effective July 17, 2026

This SMS Privacy Policy explains how Bejarano Insurance Agency LLC ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with our text-message (SMS) program. It supplements any other privacy notice we provide.

Mobile information is never shared or sold

We do not sell, rent, or share mobile phone numbers or SMS consent with any third parties or affiliates for their own marketing or promotional purposes. Phone numbers collected for text messaging are used solely by our agency to service your insurance policies. Information may be shared only with the vendors that operate our messaging on our behalf (for example, our SMS provider), and only as needed to deliver these messages.

What messages you may receive

If you have a policy with our agency and have provided your mobile number, we may send you account and service notifications — such as billing reminders, payment-due and pending-cancellation notices, and policy renewal reminders. These are transactional, account-related messages, not marketing.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies based on your account activity. You will generally receive messages only when there is a time-sensitive matter on your policy, such as a payment due date or an upcoming renewal.

Message and data rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan. We are not responsible for charges imposed by your carrier.

How to opt out or get help

You can cancel text messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. After you reply STOP, we will send one confirmation and will not send further texts unless you opt back in. For help, reply HELP or call us at (626) 210-2478. Opting out of texts does not affect your policy or other ways we contact you.
