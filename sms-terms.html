Program description

Bejarano Insurance Agency LLC operates a text-message (SMS) program to send account and service notifications to clients — including billing reminders, payment-due and pending-cancellation notices, and policy renewal reminders. By providing your mobile number and consenting, you agree to these Terms.

How you enroll

You are enrolled when you provide your mobile number to our agency — verbally or in writing — while purchasing or servicing an insurance policy, and consent to receive account notifications. These are transactional messages related to your policy, not marketing.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies based on your account activity. You will generally receive messages only when there is a time-sensitive matter on your policy, such as a payment due date or an upcoming renewal.

Cost

Message and data rates may apply. Your mobile carrier's standard messaging and data charges apply to each message, based on your plan. We do not charge a fee for the messages themselves.

Opt out and help

Reply STOP to any message to cancel at any time. You will receive one confirmation, after which no further texts will be sent unless you opt back in. Reply HELP for assistance, or call (626) 210-2478.

Supported carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. Message delivery is subject to your carrier's network and your device.
